## 1. Jared Robin

**Role:** Co-Founder, RevGenius  
**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/jaredrobin/  

### Summary
Jared Robin built RevGenius from a single LinkedIn post into a large B2B GTM community. His work focuses on community as a revenue driver and go-to-market strategy.

### Key Articles

- **Why Community-Led Growth is SaaS' Last Hope**  
  https://www.revgenius.com/mag/community-led-growth-saas  
  **Jared Robin — CLG Insight:**  
  Community-led growth outperforms traditional demand generation because buyers trust peer interactions more than brand messaging.

- **Is Your Brand Ready For Community-Led Growth?**  
  https://www.revgenius.com/mag/is-your-brand-ready-for-community-led-growth/  
  **Jared Robin — CLG Insight:**  
  Community can directly generate pipeline and revenue when aligned with GTM strategy.

- **Community 101: Guide to Building, Nurturing, and Sustaining Community**  
  https://www.revgenius.com/mag/community-101-guide  
  **Jared Robin — CLG Insight:**  
  Strong communities require clear purpose, governance, and continuous value creation to sustain engagement.

Community must be mission-driven and people-first, with monetization as a secondary outcome.

## Shared Patterns Across All Three

- Community must be built around a **shared mission or identity**, not just a product  
- Trust and peer interaction drive stronger outcomes than traditional marketing  
- CLG impacts **retention, referrals, and pipeline**, not just acquisition  
- Sustainable growth requires **clear strategy and structured execution**
